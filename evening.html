Our Favourite Evening Dates

Duck and Waffle

Come to the Duck and Waffle to enjoy spectacular views of London whilst sitting in a beautifully decorated restaurant. The Duck and Waffle offers traditional English cuisine with broad European influences which are complimented by a vast cocktail menu. This would be a perfect experience for couples looking to splash out a little more and dress up for a classy night. We recommend you book a few months in advance for a date at this restaurant as bookings go extremely quickly and you can only book up to two months in advance.

Duck and Waffle, 110 Bishopsgate, London, EC2N 4AY. Open 24/7 Monday to Friday. Contact number 0203 640 7310.
Check out their website for more details.

West End Show

The vast range of West End shows currently being performed at the moment make this a perfect night out for couples. No matter how different your tastes, you will find that there is a show for everyone. With productions ranging from Disney to caboret, from horror to historical, you cannot go wrong with a trip to the West End.Tickets for shows can be pricey so we recommend you book well in advance. However be on the look out for deals that are always popping up enabling you to cop a deal on tickets for under half the price!

Visit the London Theatre website for more details on the shows currently being performed.

Ronnie Scott's Jazz Club

Opened by Ronnie Scott himself in 1959, this club has since established itself one of the world's most famous jazz clubs. Whilst the club isn't as small as some, it still holds the same cosy atmosphere, making a romantic setting for a more intimate date night.If you're on a budget you can check out the upstairs of the club-Ronnie's Bar-for cheaper themed nights. Upstairs they have a more eclectic range of DJ sets including Latin, jive, blues, flamenco and even tap dancing. Whether you're jamming to the jazz music downstairs of dancing the night away upstairs, there's no end of fun at Ronnie Scott's.

The club is located 47 Frith Steet, Soho, London, W1D 4HT. Check out their website for more information.
