Special Occasion

The Shard

Take a trip up to The Shard! This 95-storey beast lies in the heart of Southwark and is the ideal spot to celebrate a special moment with your special someone. Soak in the surreal view of London whilst dining at a selection of restaurants (our personal favourite is Duck and Waffle - it’s open 24/7!). The Shard is located within a few minutes’ walk from London Bridge Underground Station (Northern and Jubilee lines) and London Bridge Station (Southern and South Eastern trains) – you can’t miss it. Don’t forget to grab your dancing shoes as every Saturday night 10PM TimeOut throws a silent disco on the top floor!

Our recommendations

  • Pack your camera to capture some of the most breathtaking views London has to offer
  • Visit one of the equisite, world-class restaurants from a variety of cuisines.
  • Pop over for some extra cool events like the silent disco!

32 London Bridge St, London SE1 9SG Open 10: am until 10pm.

Circus

Circus is a beautiful collision of cabaret restaurant and cocktail bar nestling in the heart of London’s vibrant Covent Garden. Join them for a night of exquisite entertainment whilst dining their highly popular Pan-Asian menu, created and overseen by Executive Chef Andrew Lassetter.
The stylish surrealist interior transports guests into a decadent world of glamour and escapism from the moment they enter, with the highlight; a striking quartz 'Stage' table seating up to 28 guests dominating the room. As evening descends, the restaurant comes to life and the spectacle begins as diners sip exotic punches and cocktails whilst beautifully skilled performers twirl magically in suspended hoops and wow the crowd. The adventures continue after dinner as the music is turned up and the dancing begins.

Our recommendations

  • Dress to impress!
  • Get your camera ready to take some jaw-dropping pictures

Full address: 27-29 Endell St, London WC2H 9BA
Open daily from 6pm - 1am. Closed Sunday and Monday.

Check out thier website for more details.


Afternoon Tea on the River Thames

Why not see the best views in London while you tuck into tea, scones and all the trimmings of a traditional afternoon tea on the Thames? Afternoon Tea while cruising along the Thames. What a civilised way to pass the afternoon. And if the mood takes you, perhaps a glass of something bubbly?

Our recommendations

  • Bring a coat and camera to take in the London Landmarks.
  • Visit their website to book your tickets.

The cruise departs daily from Tower Pier. The boat leaves at 15.30 (boarding at 15.15) from Tower Pier and returns to Tower pier at 17.00.
