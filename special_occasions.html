Special Occasion

The Shard

Take a trip up to The Shard! This 95-storey beast lies in the heart of Southwark and is the ideal spot to celebrate a special moment with your special someone. Soak in the surreal view of London whilst dining at a selection of restaurants (our personal favourite is Duck and Waffle - it’s open 24/7!). Don’t forget to grab your dancing shoes as every Saturday night 10PM TimeOut throws a silent disco on the top floor!

32 London Bridge St, London SE1 9SG.Open 10: am until 10pm. Check out their website for more details.

Circus

Circus is a beautiful collision of cabaret restaurant and cocktail bar nestling in the heart of London’s vibrant Covent Garden. Join them for a night of exquisite entertainment whilst dining their highly popular Pan-Asian menu. As evening descends, the restaurant comes to life as diners sip exotic punches and cocktails whilst beautifully skilled performers twirl magically in suspended hoops and wow the crowd.

Full address: 27-29 Endell St, London WC2H 9BA. Open daily from 6pm - 1am. Closed Sunday and Monday. Check out their website for more details.

Afternoon Tea on the Thames

Why not see the best views in London while you tuck into tea, scones and all the trimmings of a traditional afternoon tea on the Thames? Afternoon Tea while cruising along the Thames. What a civilised way to pass the afternoon. And if the mood takes you, perhaps a glass of something bubbly?

The cruise departs daily from Tower Pier. The boat leaves at 15.30 (boarding at 15.15) from Tower Pier and returns to Tower pier at 17.00. Check out this website for more details.
